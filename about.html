Our Story

FolioGenie was founded with a simple mission: to streamline the complex world of financial services for Independent Financial Advisors and Wealth Managers. We recognized that the industry was fragmented, with multiple platforms and providers creating inefficiencies and increasing costs.

Today, we provide a unified platform that aggregates services, simplifies contracts, and reduces operational overhead, allowing financial professionals to focus on what matters most - serving their clients.
